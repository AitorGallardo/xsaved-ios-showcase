# Roadmap

The app is in TestFlight beta and under active development. Dates are intentions, not promises.

## Shipped

- The library: an image-forward grid, search, and a detail view with media, notes, and tags.
- System integrations: saving from the share sheet, voice, and search; a home-screen widget.
- Live sync: sign in, pull your real library, and have new saves arrive on their own.
- A performance pass so the grid stays smooth at any size, with light, careful media loading.

## Next

- Stronger search, to match what's available on the desktop.
- A richer on-device store, with bookmark enrichment after a quick save.

## Later

- Dark mode (the design system is already built for it).
- Smarter browsing — finding bookmarks by meaning, not just words.

## Not planned

- A web wrapper. If something can't be done natively and well, it waits.
- Doing the heavy collection work on the phone — that stays elsewhere, so the app stays fast.
